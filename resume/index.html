About Me

I'm a seasoned web and user experience designer who enjoys solving problems, organizing information, and designing interactions that support and enrich people's experiences online. I've helped start-ups, non-profit organizations, higher education institutions, retailers, and big brands work better on the web.

Highlights

  • PUMA

    Working alongside PUMA's marketing team, I re- architected the company's sports/lifestyle-driven sites with a flexible, but consistent design library that can focus on each site's individual goals, visual brand, content and consumer relationships.
